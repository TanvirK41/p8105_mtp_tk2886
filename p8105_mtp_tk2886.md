EEOP is enlarged external occipital protuberance and it is *defined* as
individuals who have **exceeded 10mm in size for their bony
outgrowths**. I used the variable eop\_size\_mm. I filtered that

size. The calculated prevalence of **EEOP** in the study population is
**32.10%** which is similar to the prevalence reported by the author
**33%**.
